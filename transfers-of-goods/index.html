Task

The business requested a solution to the fraud issue during transfers between dark stores, which caused the company to lose money and goods

How the process works

• A dark store administrator creates a shipping task, specifies the items, then hands them over to the courier and completes the task.

• The administrator at the receiving dark store collects the goods from the courier and starts the receiving process.

• During the process, they record damage and discrepancies in quantity.

• If violations are found, the items in the sending dark store are blocked from sale and must be recounted to verify the accuracy of stock data.

Busines pain points

Goods intended for shipping disappear from the storefront before they are actually sent
Discrepancies between shipped and received goods are not tracked
Shipping tasks are created but not dispatched
Have to reach for the button to complete the order

Pain points of inventory specialists and dark store administrators

Send an incorrect quantity of goods
There is no visibility into delivery timelines
Send items that are not included in the request
Forced to accept defective goods and write them off as their own losses
Extra items cannot be added, and this affects stock balances
The app does not indicate that the item is already being shipped
The app does not assist with address selection
The app does not remind users about shipments
